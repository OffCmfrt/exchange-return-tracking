# Multi-Product Selection Guide

## 🎯 How to Select Multiple Products at Once

The product inventory interface now supports **bulk selection** and **multi-product shipment creation**!

---

## ✅ Option 1: Select Individual Products

1. **Navigate to** Product Inventory tab in Admin Panel
2. **Search** for products (optional)
3. **Click the checkbox** in the top-left corner of any product card
4. **Repeat** for each product you want to select
5. Selected products will have a **highlighted border**

---

## ✅ Option 2: Select All Products

1. **Navigate to** Product Inventory tab
2. **Click "Select All"** checkbox in the bulk toolbar
3. **All products** on the current page will be selected instantly

---

## 📦 Create Bulk Shipment

Once you've selected products:

1. **Check the count** in the toolbar ("X products selected")
2. **Click "Assign Selected to Ambassador"** button
3. **Fill in the modal**:
   - Select Ambassador (required)
   - Set Sent Date (required)
   - Reel Due Date (optional - leave blank for inventory-only shipments)
   - Shipping Address (auto-fills from ambassador profile, editable)
   - Mark as Monthly Target (yes/no)
   - Notes (optional)

4. **Click "Create Shipment for X Products"**
5. **Done!** All selected products will be shipped to the ambassador

---

## 🔄 Quick Actions

| Action | How To |
|--------|--------|
| **Select one product** | Click checkbox on product card |
| **Select all products** | Click "Select All" in toolbar |
| **Deselect one product** | Uncheck the product checkbox |
| **Deselect all** | Uncheck "Select All" |
| **Assign single product** | Click "Assign Single" button on card |
| **Assign multiple** | Select products → Click "Assign Selected" |

---

## 🎨 Visual Indicators

- **Selected products**: Black border + glow effect
- **Unselected products**: Normal border
- **Toolbar**: Shows when products are loaded
- **Count badge**: Updates in real-time

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ **Search first** to filter products before selecting  
✅ **Use "Select All"** for full-page assignments  
✅ **Shipping address auto-fills** from ambassador profile  
✅ **Reel due date is optional** for inventory shipments  
✅ **All products share the same shipping address**  

---

## 📊 Example Workflow

**Scenario**: Send 5 products to ambassador @fashionista

1. Search "summer collection"
2. Click checkboxes on 5 products
3. Click "Assign Selected to Ambassador"
4. Select @fashionista from dropdown
5. Address auto-fills ✅
6. Leave reel due date blank (inventory only)
7. Click "Create Shipment for 5 Products"
8. **Success!** 🎉

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Selections reset when you search or change pages
- Each product creates a separate shipment record
- All products in bulk shipment go to the same address
- You can still assign single products using "Assign Single" button
